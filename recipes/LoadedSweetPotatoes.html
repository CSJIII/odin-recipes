Loaded Sweet Potatoes

Recipe Description

This recipe will show you how to make a vegan friendly Loaded Sweet Potato. This potato is packed with many different vegan friendly ingredients such as kale and black beans. The recipe will also require you to make a homemade green goddess dressing to add some kick to the potato.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium sweet potatoes
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 bunch kale, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • Green Goddess Dressing to serve
  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Line baking sheet with parchment paper.
  3. Using a fork, poke multiple holes into sweet potatoes.
  4. Place potatoes in oven and bake for about 45-60 minutes, or until tender.
  5. In the meantime, heat olive oil over medium-high heat in medium saucepan
  6. Add garlic and cook for 60 seconds, or until fragrant.
  7. Add kale and toss to coat.
  8. Add 1/4 cup water and cover for 3-5 minutes.
  9. Remove cover, toss kale, reduce heat to low, and cook for another 10-15 minutes, or until kale is desired texture adding more water as needed.
  10. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  11. Cut sweet potatoes in half lengthwise.
